## The Core Model

- It's evolving, learning, growing — just like we are

### Individuated Units of Consciousness (IUOCs)

- **We are these.** You. Me. Every conscious being.
- We are not separate from the LCS — we are *individuated* pieces of it
- Like cells in a body — individual but part of the whole
- Immortal — consciousness doesn't die when the body does
- Each IUOC has a quality level measured by entropy (see below)

### The Virtual Reality Frame

- Our physical universe is a **virtual reality** rendered by the LCS
- Not metaphorically — literally a computed simulation with rules
- Physics (gravity, electromagnetism, etc.) = the rules of the game
- Time = a rendering parameter, not fundamental
- Space = another rendering parameter
- Our bodies = avatars. We are the players.
- The LCS sends each IUOC a **data stream** that we interpret as experience

### Physical Matter Reality (PMR) vs Non-Physical Matter Reality (NPMR)

| Frame | What It Is | Rules |
|---|---|---|
| **PMR** | Our physical universe — the "game" we're currently playing | Physics, time, space, cause-and-effect |
| **NPMR** | The larger reality — where IUOCs exist between "rounds" | Different rules, no physical constraints |

We exist in both simultaneously. PMR is where we experience avatars. NPMR is our "native" state.

- A high-quality consciousness operates with low entropy
- Choices motivated by **love, caring, cooperation** = low entropy
- Choices motivated by **fear, selfishness, ego** = high entropy
- Every choice you make either raises or lowers your entropy
- Over many "lives" (rounds of the game), your IUOC evolves toward lower entropy

```
Quality of Consciousness = f(Intent of Choices)

Love/Caring/Cooperation → Low Entropy → Higher Quality → Evolution
Fear/Selfishness/Ego → High Entropy → Lower Quality → Degradation
```

**This is not morality. It's physics.** Low entropy is objectively more efficient, more coherent, more functional. The system literally works better when its parts cooperate.

## Why Are We Here? The Purpose of Life

Life exists because the LCS needs to evolve, and evolution requires **making choices in a constrained environment.**

### The Training Ground

1. The LCS creates a virtual reality (our universe) with specific rules
2. IUOCs "log in" by incarnating into avatars (bodies)
3. The constrained environment forces choices — you can't avoid choosing
4. Each choice reveals and shapes the quality of your consciousness
5. Results feed back to the IUOC — learning happens
6. Over many iterations, entropy decreases, quality increases
7. The entire LCS evolves as its parts evolve

**Life is a school. Not punishment — opportunity.**

### Why Suffering Exists

- Suffering = high-entropy choices playing out (cause and effect)
- It's not punishment — it's feedback
- The system shows you the consequences of your choices
- Suffering motivates change — it's the "pain" that teaches
- The goal isn't to eliminate suffering but to learn from it and evolve past it

### Free Will

- Free will is real — you are genuinely choosing
- But it's constrained by the rules of the virtual reality
- You can't violate physics in PMR, but you CAN choose your intent
- The quality of your intent is the only thing you fully control
- That's why it's the metric the system measures
